The Mackes Audio Platform (MAP)2

It is a Guitar (or Any Instrument) multi channel Effects Processor with NAM,IR and LV2 Support
It is a Drum Machine, Brain, Sequencer
It is a Keyboard MIDI Brain, Sequencer
It is Multi Channel, Multi Track, Multi Workload, Multi Node, with Failover.
It excels at taking ordinary MIDI devices and making the awesome
It is a AVB Talker and Listener
It has extensive methods to track changes, confirm and control audio workflows (Single Source of Truth, No Split Brain)

It is an RPM installed on Fedora Linux (server)
It is Mostly React, Textual, and Python scripts + JUCE
It leverages standard Fedora Packages for EVERYTHING else.
It is a set of Clustered Services (If you choose)
It is a stand alone (Single PC) if you choose
